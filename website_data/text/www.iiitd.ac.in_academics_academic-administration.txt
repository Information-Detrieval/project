Academic Administration
The mission of the Department of Academic Administration at IIITD is to support the University in its primary duties of teaching, research and service through
The provision, maintenance and application of effective administrative procedures.
The provision of a fast and error-free service to Faculty, Students, Parents of the students, the relevant government institutions and the broader public.
