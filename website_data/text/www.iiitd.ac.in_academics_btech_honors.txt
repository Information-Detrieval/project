Honors Program
IIIT-Delhi has provision for an Honors program which is designed to challenge the brighter and more ambitious students, without burdening an average student. For details, refer to the B.Tech. Regulations. Some salient features are listed below:
The student must complete an additional 12 discipline credits.
The student must do a B.Tech. Project.
At graduation time, the student must have a CGPA of 8.0 or more.
