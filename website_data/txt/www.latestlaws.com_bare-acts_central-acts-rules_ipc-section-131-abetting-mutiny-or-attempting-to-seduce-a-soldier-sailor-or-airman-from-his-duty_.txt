Of Offences Relating To The Army 
-
Parliament of India
Whoever abets the committing of mutiny by an officer, soldier, 1[sailor or airman], in the Army, 2[Navy or Air Force] of the 3[Government of India] or attempts to seduce any such officer, soldier, 1[sailor or airman] from his allegiance or his duty, shall be punished with 4[imprisonment for life], or with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
5[Explanation.—In this section the words “officer”, 6[“soldier”, 7[“sailor”] and “airman”] include any person subject to the 8[Army Act, 9[the Army Act, 1950 (46 of 1950)], 10[the Naval Discipline Act, 11***the 11***the 12Indian Navy (Discipline) Act,1934 (34 of 1934)] 13[the Air Force Act or 14[the Air Force Act,  1950 (45 of 1950)]], as the case may be].]
