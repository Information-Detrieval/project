Verification of UG / PG Students
Procedure to obtain verification of UG / PG Transcripts, Degrees, Certificates etc.
Write an application by email or post directly to the address given below along with proof of payment of fee, as applicable. Your Application should provide the following facts:
Your Company Name along with office address and contact no.
Student Name, Institute Roll No. and Program of Study
Document / Details to be verified: Transcript/Degree/Any other document or Information
Copy of the document to be verified (If Available)

Fee/Payment Details
Mention the Total Amount (INR) as 1180 (Rs.1000 + 18% GST) and click on the check box of “I agree to the Terms of Payment.” using payment link
Please follow the below process for making the payment:-
Fill up your details (Name, E-mail ID, Address, Pin code, Mobile No.) as asked in the form.
Under “Select Payment Purpose” select Education Verfication Fees.
Mention the Total Amount (INR) as 1180 and click on the check box of “I agree to the Terms of Payment.”
Click on “Pay Now”
