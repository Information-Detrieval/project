Dining Services
 
Brew Bakes
              Brew Bakes is fully operational at the institute, located at GF of old academic building. The time of operation is from 8.00 a.m to 8.00 p.m from Monday to Saturday. The menu includes wide range of Coffee, Sandwiches, Pizza, Burgers etc. Facility to cater parties, small gatherings is also available and can be reached at Ext. 599.
              
 
 
DOSA Inc.
            "DOSA Inc." is situated at the second floor of the student center building. It caters different varirties of DOSA's, Uttapams, Idly's etc.  
          
 
Mother Diary
          An outlet of Mother Diary is also functional now at the ground floor of the Student Centre. It provides milk and a variety of milk products like yogurt, lassi, paneer, milkshakes, ice creams, etc. At present operating between Monday to Saturday from 7.00 AM to 8.00 PM  
        
 
 
Dining Area
        There are 2 mess services available at the first & second floor of the student center. The canteen along with Kodechef and DOSA Inc are also situated at the ground floor.  
      
 
 
 
Mess Timing
Breakfast - 7.30 AM - 10.00 AM
Lunch - 12.30 PM - 2.30 PM
Evening snacks -  5.00 PM - 6.00 PM
Dinner -  8.00 PM - 9.30 PM
