Getting Around Taj
I Love Agra Point
It's a popular selfie point located just about 2.00kms from Taj Mahal eastern gate, it's a tri crossing open area on Fatehabad road having a stage depicting I Love Agra at the back drop where time to time cultural programs, band presentations are being organized, entire area is beautifully landscaped, local eateries & frilled shops attract the people to this popular area.
Approx. Distance/travel time-Hotel Taj & Convention Centre - 0.75km / 1min / Hotel Taj View - 2.5km/3min.
ZONAL PARKCHAUPATI
Located at Taj Nagari phase-2 around 3.00 km from Taj Mahal Eastern gate is a beautifully landscaped park having a standard amphitheatre specially designed for Indian classical dances. At one corner, a series of eateries serving various delicious cuisines of Agra (local) as well as other parts of the country. Grassy expanse and swings in the park attracts children to this place. A wonderful place to enjoy morning walks and evenings with family.
